## Built an Interactive Smart Logistics Dashboard in Excel (Using a Kaggle Dataset)

I completed a data analysis project where I worked with a logistics dataset downloaded from Kaggle and transformed it into an interactive Excel dashboard.

👉 The first step was cleaning the dataset to make it suitable for analysis which involved handling missing values, removing duplicates, and organizing the data into a structured format. After the data was clean, 
👉 I calculated key performance indicators (KPIs) that reflect how logistics operations are performing.
The KPIs I created include:
 🫤 The total shipment
 🫤 Average waiting time
 🫤 Asset utilization percentage
 🫤 Delay rate
 🫤 Inventory surplus

These KPI helped raw data move beyond ordinary data and actually understand the operational performance of the system.

For example, the delay rate shows how efficient the logistics process is, while asset utilization helps identify how effectively trucks are being used. The inventory surplus KPI also helped highlight the difference between demand forecast and available inventory.

👉 Then I built an interactive dashboard that includes shipment status analysis, delay reasons, truck utilization, traffic vs logistics delay, and demand forecast vs inventory trends.

## 1. Shipment Status Chart (Delayed, Delivered, In Transit)
This shows how shipments are performing at a glance and helps the company see if most shipments are delivered on time or delayed.
If the “Delayed” section is large, the company immediately knows there is an operational problem that needs attention.

## 2. Traffic vs Logistics Delay Chart
This shows how traffic conditions affect waiting time and delays and helps identify whether traffic is a major cause of delay
If heavy traffic leads to longer waiting time the company can change delivery routes and adjust delivery schedules

## 3. Truck Utilization Chart
This shows how efficiently each truck (asset) is being used and helps compare truck performance. If some trucks are used more than others, the company can Balance the workload, Reduce maintenance cost, 

## 4. Delay Reasons Chart (Traffic, Weather, Mechanical Failure, etc.)
This shows the main causes of delays and helps the company focus on the biggest problem first Instead of guessing, the company can see If traffic is the biggest issue, If equipment problems are increasing

## 5. Demand Forecast vs Inventory Chart
This compares how much the company expects to ship vs what they actually have in inventory and helps detect surplus or shortage
This helps the company to Avoid overstocking,Avoid running out of inventory,Improve planning
